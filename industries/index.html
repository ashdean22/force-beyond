Industries We Serve

Delivering precision-manufactured components to the world's most demanding industries.

Aerospace

Turbine engine components, structural airframe parts, fasteners, and exhaust systems in Inconel, titanium, and other superalloys.

Oil & Gas

Subsea equipment, valve bodies, downhole tools, wellhead components, and pump parts in corrosion-resistant alloys.

Power Generation

Gas turbine hot-section components, steam turbine parts, heat exchangers, and valve assemblies for conventional and nuclear power.

Nuclear & SMR

Reactor vessel internals, control rod mechanisms, heat exchangers, and safety-critical structural components.

Automotive

High-performance turbocharger components, exhaust manifolds, transmission parts, and precision-machined engine components.

Mining

Wear-resistant pump components, valve bodies, crusher parts, and material handling equipment in hardened alloys.

Medical

Titanium and cobalt-chrome orthopedic implants, surgical instruments, and precision medical device components.

Marine & Subsea

Propeller components, seawater system parts, and subsea connectors in corrosion-resistant Inconel and titanium alloys.

Chemical Processing

Reactor vessels, heat exchangers, piping components, and valve trim in alloys resistant to acids and chlorides.

Defense

Mission-critical structural components, armor system parts, and specialized hardware for military applications.
